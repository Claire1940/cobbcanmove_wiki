<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Level 2 Sensory Rule Introduction">
    The v1.4 update ensures Level 2 always includes a sensory rule (hear, smell, or see). This is the point where Cobb transitions from a passive threat to an actively reactive one, demanding heightened awareness from players.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Death Tips">
    After v1.4, dying now displays helpful tips. These hints are invaluable for understanding the active rule and identifying mistakes, preventing repeated failures on the same route. Read them carefully before restarting.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Strategic Rock Usage in Level 7">
    The v1.5 update added rocks to Level 7. With later patches making rocks more effective for stunning Cobb, players should actively scout for and use throwables instead of solely focusing on coal and batteries.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Rebalanced Final Task">
    The v1.4 patch adjusted the final task text after all four batteries are placed, and slightly reduced Cobb's speed. The v1.5.2 update then slightly buffed Cobb's speed, maintaining a balanced and tense finale.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Easier Ending Furnace Interaction">
    The v1.6 update enlarged the blast furnace hitbox in the ending. This change makes it less likely for players to lose the furnace behind other objects during the high-pressure final sequence, ensuring smoother interaction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Unlock Level Select">
    Upon successfully beating the game once, v1.5 adds a level select menu. This allows players to replay any story-mode level, perfect for practicing difficult sections, re-experiencing specific rule combinations, or mastering the ending.
  </Step>
</Steps>

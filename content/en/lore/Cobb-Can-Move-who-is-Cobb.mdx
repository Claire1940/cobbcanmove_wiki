<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Tactics Against Cobb's Abilities">
Surviving against Cobb requires a combination of awareness, strategic movement, and effective use of environmental elements. Here are key tactics to employ:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Read the Rules Carefully">
    At the start of each run, a new rule is introduced. This rule dictates Cobb's primary method of detection or movement. **Understanding this rule** is the single most important survival tip.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Movement and Stealth">
    If Cobb can see, stay in shadows or behind cover. If Cobb can hear, move slowly and avoid unnecessary actions. If Cobb can smell, consider your route to avoid lingering in its path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Rocks for Distraction or Stun">
    Rocks are not just clutter. They can be thrown to create distractions, drawing Cobb away from your path. More importantly, a direct hit from a rock can briefly stun Cobb, providing a crucial window to escape.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Resources Efficiently">
    Coal is essential for the furnace, but collecting it can expose you. Plan your coal routes to minimize contact with Cobb, especially when sensory rules are active. Carrots can respawn, providing a renewable food source to counter starvation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Adapt to Balance Changes">
    The developer frequently updates Cobb's behavior. For instance, v1.7 nerfed "Cobb can smell" and slightly reduced its long-pursuit acceleration, while v1.6 improved rock stunning reliability. Staying informed about patches can refine your tactics.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Effective Rock Usage">
Rocks became a more reliable defensive tool after v1.7 fixed issues where they sometimes failed to stun Cobb during pursuit. Use them strategically as a last resort or to create escape opportunities.
</Callout>

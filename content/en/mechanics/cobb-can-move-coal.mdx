<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Interacting with Other Key Objects">
Beyond coal and furnaces, several other items play crucial roles in your survival and progression within Cobb Can Move. Understanding their utility is vital for a successful run.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Rocks: Utility & Distraction">
    Rocks are throwable items that can be used to distract Cobb. In v1.6, direct hits with rocks could briefly stun Cobb, and their sound was made louder for clearer feedback. v1.7 further fixed instances where rocks failed to stun Cobb during pursuit, making them a more reliable defensive tool.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Carrots: Renewable Food Source">
    After the v1.3 update, carrots respawn when eaten, making them a repeatable item source for managing the player's "starve" mechanic. This ensures you're not constantly searching for new food sources.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Batteries: Ending Progression">
    Batteries are critical for the ending sequence and the final task. There are four batteries to collect, and the final task text changes once all of them are placed. Tracking your battery progress is essential for completing the game.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Breaker: Map Visibility">
    The breaker is an interactable map object. In v1.3, its sprite was slightly changed to make it more noticeable, helping players recognize it quickly during a run. Knowing its location can sometimes be crucial.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="info">
Item swapping is more forgiving now. The v1.4 update allows players to swap items even if the drop spot is over a wall and shown in red, reducing inventory management frustration.

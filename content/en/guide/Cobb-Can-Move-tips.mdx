<Section icon={Target} title="Essential Beginner Survival Tips for Cobb Can Move">
For new players, the initial runs in Cobb Can Move can be daunting. Understanding the basic survival loop and how to react to Cobb's evolving threats is crucial. These tips will help you navigate your first few levels and minimize rookie mistakes.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Read the Rule Text Every Run">
    The core of Cobb Can Move is its dynamic rule system. Before starting a level, always check the active rule text. This rule dictates how Cobb will behave and what sensory abilities he possesses for that specific run. For instance, v1.4 guarantees that Level 2 will introduce a sensory rule like "Cobb can hear," "Cobb can smell," or "Cobb can see."
    <Callout type="warning">
    Ignoring the active rule is a common beginner mistake. Each rule changes the threat profile; don't approach every level with the same strategy.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Move Carefully Around Cobb's Senses">
    Cobb's sensory rules directly impact how he detects your presence. If "Cobb can see" is active, avoid his line of sight and use darkness to your advantage. If "Cobb can smell" is active, your movement might leave a scent trail. Recent updates (v1.4, v1.7) have balanced these senses, with v1.7 specifically nerfing "Cobb can smell" to be less persistent.
    <Callout type="tip">
    Treat sight and smell rules as immediate danger indicators. Maintain extra distance and plan your routes to minimize exposure to Cobb's active senses.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Items Strategically">
    Items like coal, rocks, and carrots are vital for survival. Coal fuels the furnace, a primary objective. Rocks can be thrown to distract or, after v1.6 and v1.7 updates, even stun Cobb temporarily on a direct hit. Carrots, since v1.3, respawn when eaten, providing a repeatable food source.
    <Callout type="info">
    Item swapping is more forgiving since v1.4, allowing you to swap items even if the drop spot is over a wall. This helps in managing your inventory efficiently.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Learn from Death Tips">
    Dying in Cobb Can Move isn't just a failure; it's a learning opportunity. The v1.4 update added helpful tips that display after each death. These tips often highlight the mistake you made or provide guidance on the active rule.
    <Callout type="success">
    Always read the death tip before restarting. It's designed to prevent you from repeating the same errors and helps you understand the game's mechanics faster.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Strategic Approach for Level 7 Survival">
To successfully navigate Level 7, a methodical approach focusing on resource management, environmental awareness, and effective use of items is essential. Cobb's heightened aggression and the complex map layout demand careful planning and execution.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Prioritize Rock Scouting">
    Upon entering Level 7, immediately scout for rocks. With the v1.5 update, rocks were specifically added to this level, making them a critical resource. Rocks can briefly stun Cobb on a direct hit, providing precious seconds to escape or complete an objective. Their sound is also louder now, making them easier to locate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Coal and Battery Routing">
    Coal management remains central. While v1.6 made coal less likely to spawn directly next to the furnace, efficient routing between coal deposits and the furnace is vital. Simultaneously, track the four batteries required for the final task. Plan your movements to collect these without drawing unnecessary attention from Cobb.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Utilize Death Tips for Learning">
    If you die, pay close attention to the death tips introduced in v1.4. These tips offer specific insights into what went wrong or what rule was active, helping you adapt your strategy for the next attempt. Each failure becomes a learning opportunity.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Adapt to Cobb's Sensory Rules">
    Level 7 often combines multiple challenging rules. Be prepared for "Cobb can see," "Cobb can smell," or "Cobb can hear." Adjust your playstyle accordingly:
    - **See**: Avoid bright areas and use shadows.
    - **Smell**: Move quickly and avoid lingering in one spot.
    - **Hear**: Minimize noisy actions and movement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Prepare for the Final Task">
    Once all four batteries are placed, the final task text changes, initiating the ending sequence. Cobb's speed during this final task has been rebalanced (nerfed slightly in v1.4, then buffed in v1.5.2), but remains intense. The v1.6 update also enlarged the blast furnace hitbox, making the final interaction less prone to error.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Out for Long Pursuits">
Cobb can get slightly faster during long pursuits, and in larger maps, his wandering speed increases (v1.6). While v1.7 slightly nerfed his acceleration during long pursuits, extended chases are still highly dangerous. Avoid getting cornered or spending too much time in open areas.
</Callout>

</Section>

  <Card title="Breakers: The Unseen Trigger">

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Conquering the Ending Sequence">
Beating Level 7 leads directly into the game's ending sequence, which has also seen several balance adjustments. Understanding these changes is key to successfully completing Cobb Can Move.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Four Batteries and Final Task">
    The core of the ending involves placing four batteries. Once all are placed, the final task text changes. This signals the start of the true finale, where Cobb's pursuit becomes relentless.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Cobb's Rebalanced Speed">
    The v1.4 patch initially reduced Cobb’s speed during the final task, making it slightly less frantic. However, v1.5.2 later buffed his speed slightly, ensuring the finale remains tense and challenging without being overtly unfair. Be prepared for a fast-paced chase.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Interacting with the Blast Furnace">

  <Step num="4" title="Unlocking Level Select">
    After successfully beating the game once, v1.5 adds a level select menu. This feature is invaluable for replaying Level 7, practicing the ending, or experimenting with specific rule combinations without having to restart from Level 1.
  </Step>

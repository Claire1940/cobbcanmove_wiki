<Section icon={Target} title="Survival Mechanics and Rule Systems">

The core of the game is its dynamic rule system. On all **Cobb Can Move platforms**, the game generates a random rule at the start of each level. You must adapt your playstyle immediately based on the text displayed at the top of the screen.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Sensory Rules">
    - **Cobb Can See**: Visibility matters.
    - **Cobb Can Hear**: Noise is fatal.
    - **Cobb Can Smell**: Proximity is tracked.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Movement Rules">
    - **Cobb Can Reach**: Larger steps.
    - **Cobb Can Move**: Standard pursuit.
    - **Duplicate**: Multiple Cobbs appear.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Player Constraints">
    - **Freeze**: Stay near light.
    - **Starve**: Collect carrots.
    - **Darkness**: Limited field of view.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

Survival depends on your ability to recognize these rules. For instance, if the rule is "Cobb Can Smell," hiding behind a wall won't save you for long. You must keep moving to stay ahead of his tracking.

| Rule Name | Player Strategy | Cobb Behavior Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cobb Can See** | Stay in shadows / Break LOS | Targets player when in light |
| **Cobb Can Hear** | Walk slowly / Use rocks | Investigates loud noises |
| **Cobb Can Smell** | Constant movement | Slowly drifts toward player |
| **Duplicate** | Monitor all directions | Spawns 2+ Cobbs after Level 5 |
| **Reach** | Maintain extra distance | Takes large, lunging steps |

<Callout type="info" title="v1.7 Balance Changes">
The latest update in May 2026 nerfed the "Cobb Can Smell" rule. Cobb now sniffs less frequently and is less persistent if he loses your trail at close range, giving you a better chance to escape.

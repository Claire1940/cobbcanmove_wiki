In the pixel horror game *Cobb Can Move*, the concept of "killing" Cobb is less about permanent elimination and more about temporary incapacitation or effective evasion. The game's core design revolves around adapting to changing rules, which directly influences how you can "deal with" Cobb. <Section icon={Sparkles} title="Effective Evasion and Survival Tactics">
Surviving against Cobb means mastering movement, resource management, and adapting to the game's unpredictable nature. The game's updates have consistently refined these aspects to ensure a challenging yet fair experience.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Read the Rule Text">
    At the start of every run, pay close attention to the active rule. This dictates Cobb's current primary detection method and how you should adjust your strategy. For example, a "Cobb can see" rule requires staying out of its line of sight, while "Cobb can hear" demands silent movement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Item Usage">
    Utilize rocks for stunning Cobb when cornered or to create a diversion. Remember that v1.7 fixed rock stun consistency, making them more reliable. Drop items strategically; v1.4 improved item swapping, allowing for quicker inventory management even in tight spots.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Control Your Movement">
    Cobb's pursuit speed increases over time (v1.6), although v1.7 slightly nerfed its acceleration. Avoid extended chases. Learn to "kite" Cobb around obstacles and through choke points to buy time. If "Cobb can smell" is active, change directions frequently to break its scent trail.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Resources Efficiently">
    Gather coal for the furnace and eat carrots to prevent starvation. These objectives often force you into dangerous areas. Plan your routes carefully to minimize exposure to Cobb's detection zones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Learn from Death Tips">
    Since v1.4, the game provides tips after you die. These are invaluable for understanding what went wrong and adapting your approach for the next run. Each death is a learning opportunity to refine your survival strategy.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Adaptation is Key">
The most successful players are those who can rapidly adapt their tactics based on the current rules and Cobb's behavior. Don't rely on a single fixed strategy.
</Callout>
